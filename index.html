3. Subscription & Billing (Auto-Renewable)

Decision Fatigue offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (“Decision Fatigue Premium”). Subscriptions are processed through Apple’s in-app purchase system.

  • Payment: Charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
  • Auto-renewal: Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Renewal charge: Your account will be charged within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
  • Manage or cancel: Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Refunds: Managed by Apple according to Apple’s refund policies.

The App uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements (premium access).

4. AI Features & User Consent

4.1 Third-Party AI Processing (xAI / Grok)

When AI features are enabled, your prompts/questions are sent to xAI’s Grok API for processing and response generation. If you enable Local Suggestions, approximate location (city-level) may be included to improve relevance.

  • Sent to xAI: AI prompts/questions, and optionally approximate location (only if Local Suggestions is enabled).
  • Not sent to xAI: Payment details, Apple ID details, or sensitive personal identifiers.

Please avoid entering sensitive personal information into AI prompts.

xAI processes data according to its own privacy practices. You can review xAI’s privacy policy here: https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy

Decision Fatigue does not train AI models using your personal data.
